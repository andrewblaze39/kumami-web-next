How it works

  • Header — Add Wallet + Browse Whales buttons (same as now)
  • Watchlist panel — always visible, shows all watched wallets. Click ⚙ Settings to expand inline controls: change threshold, mute 1h, unwatch
  • Alerts feed — read-only, no buttons ever. Just live events scrolling in. Controls are always reachable above regardless of how many alerts accumulate
  • No more chat messages — "now watching", "wallet summary" etc. don't go into the feed. The watchlist panel IS the confirmation that a wallet is being tracked
